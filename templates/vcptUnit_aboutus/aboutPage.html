What We offers

{% include 'components/topHorizontalLIne.html' %}

TESTING SERVICES

We test for indoor residual spraying (IRS), insecticidal treated nets (ITNs), skin (topical) repellents, spatial (area) repellents, treated clothing, attractive targeted sugar baits and mosquito and also develop new methods

DEVELOPMENT STRATEGIES

We develop new means of testing products and assist in developing guidelines for international use and GLP Certified since 2021

PARAMETER MEASUREMENTS

We measure key parameters in the mosquito that translate to efficacy in reducing vector borne disease in the human population when applied at scale.

DOCUMENTATION AND REPORTING WRITING

. Our documentation, analysis and reporting is rigorous for ease of use by our partners.

Also We offer protocol, data analysis and report writing services.

PROTOCOLS AND DATA ANALYSIS

We offer protocol, data analysis and report writing services.

method

We use standard WHO testing methodology (Phase I: laboratory studies, Phase II: experimental hut studies, and Phase III: community studies) for long lasting insecticidal nets and Indoor residual spray (IRS).

Good Laboratory Practice

With increasing insecticide resistance in malaria-endemic countries there is an urgent need for safe and effective novel vector control products. These tools require generation of rigorous efficacy data.

Business model

In addition to achieving GLP, the VCPTU in collaboration with IHI Grants and Finance departments has developed a business model that provides a high throughput, frictionless service for clients that encourages repeat business.

Training

The VCPTU generates large amounts of data, which provides numerous training opportunities. Currently, six PhD candidates are using product testing data as the basis of their research degrees. The unit is funding four PhDs
